Bruna CHIROPRACTIC CLINIC has been in business for 32 years. Dr. Magdalena Bruna started her practice in 1984 when she founded the facility and Dr. Silvio Bruna joined her in 1988. Since then, they have been dedicated to rendering the best service focused on your wellbeing. The doctors pride themselves in not being a high volume practice in order to give each patient the adequate time and treatment. Both are bilingual in English and Spanish and feel comfortable with patients that speak Portuguese.

Dr. Magdalena Bruna is a graduate from the University of St. Thomas in Houston, TX, where she received her Bachelor of Science Degree in 1979. She then attended Texas Chiropractic College, from where she graduated with a Doctorate in Chiropractic in 1983. She now conducts and supervises all physical therapy treatments.

Dr. Silvio Bruna is a graduate from the University of Texas in Austin receiving a Bachelor in Arts in Natural Sciences in 1982, followed by a Bachelor in Science in Medical Technology from the University of Texas, School of Allied Health Sciences, in 1983. He attended Texas Chiropractic College from where he obtained his degree of Doctor in Chiropractic in 1987. He earned a Certification as a Designated Doctor for the State of Texas in 2001 and in 2011 obtained a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Hardin Simmons University. Dr. Bruna is now the primary care giver at Bruna Chiropractic Clinic. He uses Diversified Manipulative Technique and sees patients of any age for acute and/or chronic conditions. In the near future, Dr. Bruna plans to continue furthering his education and will be able to offer Department of Transportation (DOT) Examinations for truck drivers in the State of Texas.

As a new patient, you will find yourself in a pleasant state-of-the-art facility, where you will receive a thorough consultation and evaluation along with performance and interpretation of x-ray studies of any part of the body, as needed. Your first treatment will consist of physical therapy modalities including but not limited to: ultrasound, electric muscle stimulation, and therapeutic massage before the chiropractic manipulative treatment. Other therapies may include waterbed massage, spinal traction, as well as active exercise and rehabilitation, which will be applied when necessary. A report of findings will be part of your second day of service.

The clinic is located in an area accessible within 10 minutes of I-45 North, I-610 N. Loop, I-10 and 290 NW highways, making it very accessible to patients from many areas of town. The facility opens from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM on Monday, Wednesday and Friday and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, so it may better serve the community. On these days walk-ins are welcome. On Tuesday and Thursday will accommodate office visits by appointment only. Due to working schedule, patients will be able to access care at this office after working hours.
